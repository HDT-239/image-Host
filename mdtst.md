Here is a comprehensive list of books in Open Court's **Popular Culture and Philosophy** series, compiled from the publisher's official archives and other sources. The series began in 2000 and has grown to over 125 volumes.

## 📝 Notes on the Series

- **Series Editor:** George Reisch
- **Publisher:** Open Court Publishing Company
- **First Published:** 2000
- **Volume Numbering:** Many volumes have numbers (e.g., Vol. 57 for *Rush and Philosophy*, Vol. 77 for *Boardwalk Empire and Philosophy*, Vol. 80 for *Ender's Game and Philosophy*, Vol. 107 for *Deadpool and Philosophy*, Vol. 116 for *1984 and Philosophy*, Vol. 125 for *Rick and Morty and Philosophy*, Vol. 126 for *Stranger Things and Philosophy*, Vol. 127 for *Blade Runner 2049 and Philosophy*)

The series has continued beyond 2019 with additional volumes; this list represents the most complete compilation available through 2019.
